About

Hi! I am a multi-discipline designer living and working in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. As the son of a graphic designer I was exposed to and developed a passion for design at a young age. When I was ten, Apple's original bondi blue iMac captivated my creativity and showed me that an object created with purpose and care can cause an emotional reaction in its audience.

With that lesson in my mind I earned a degree in industrial design from Auburn University while maintaining a consistent stable of freelance graphic design clients. Although my career path has taken me down a more graphic design-focused route than an industrial design or product design path, I have been happy to have many opportunities to weave the lessons in human-first intentionality and taking care to find a problem's most-correct solution into my projects.

My wife and I moved from Auburn, Alabama to the Triad area of North Carolina in 2012 and I was fortunate to work my first real design job at one of the Southeast's leading brand design firms, Mitre Agency. There I was taught a wide gamut of print production skills that have served me well and often ever since. After about a year I began working for The Variable, an up-and-coming ad agency where I've been since early 2014. The Variable has won Ad Age Small Agency of the Year Southeast — Silver in 2015, 2017, and 2018, and I'm proud to be an integral part of the agency's tightly-knit creative team.

Through all of this I have also served a wide range of clients on a freelance basis as Dan Draper Design. From Billboard-charting artists to major airlines to the mom-and-pop coffee shop next door, I am proud of the work I've done and always strive to deliver to a client a thorough, considered, correct solution.

Thank you for taking the time to look over my site. If you'd like to hear even more, are interested in doing business, or simply want to reach out and say hello, you are welcome to do so through any of the methods listed below. I am always happy to make a new connection!
